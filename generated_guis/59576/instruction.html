Coca-Cola Ambassador Resources

Fast Facts

Coca-Cola 101

The Coca-Cola Company is a total beverage company with products sold in more than 200 countries and territories. Our purpose is to refresh the world and make a difference. We offer a wide range of beverages, from sparkling soft drinks to waters, sports drinks, juices, and plant-based beverages. Founded in 1886 by John Pemberton, Coca-Cola was first served in Atlanta, Georgia.

Products & Ingredients

Our portfolio features brands like Coca-Cola, Sprite, Fanta, Dasani, Smartwater, Minute Maid, and many more. We are constantly innovating to offer more choices, including low- and no-sugar options. Ingredients vary by product but typically include carbonated water, high fructose corn syrup (or sugar), natural flavors, and phosphoric acid. We are committed to transparency about our ingredients.

Strengthening Communities

We believe in giving back to the communities where we operate. Our initiatives focus on water stewardship, women's economic empowerment, and sustainable packaging. We work with local partners to implement programs that create shared value and positively impact people's lives. This includes supporting local economies and promoting community well-being.

Diversity & Inclusion

Diversity and inclusion are fundamental to our success. We strive to create a workplace where everyone feels valued, respected, and empowered to bring their authentic selves to work. Our commitment extends to our workforce, our suppliers, our customers, and the communities we serve. We believe a diverse workforce fosters innovation and better understanding of our global consumers.

FAQ

Well-being

Q: What is Coca-Cola doing about sugar?
A: We are committed to reducing sugar across our portfolio by offering more low- and no-sugar options, developing new beverages, and introducing smaller package sizes to help people manage their sugar intake.

Q: Are your ingredients safe?
A: Yes, all ingredients used in our beverages are safe and approved by relevant regulatory authorities worldwide. We adhere to strict quality and safety standards.

Environment

Q: What is Coca-Cola's World Without Waste vision?
A: Our "World Without Waste" vision aims to collect and recycle a bottle or can for every one we sell by 2030, make our packaging 100% recyclable, and use more recycled content in our packaging.

Q: How does Coca-Cola address water scarcity?
A: We are committed to water stewardship, focusing on reducing water use in our operations, recycling water, and replenishing the water we use back to communities and nature.

Support & Contact

Q: How can I get further support as an Ambassador?
A: Please refer to your Ambassador program guide or contact your program coordinator directly. You can also find resources on the official Ambassador portal.

Q: Where can I find official company statements or press releases?
A: Visit The Coca-Cola Company's official website under the 'News & Stories' or 'Investors' sections for the latest information and press releases.

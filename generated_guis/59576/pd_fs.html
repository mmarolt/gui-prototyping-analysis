Fast Facts

Coca-Cola 101

Coca-Cola was invented by John Pemberton in 1886. It was first served at Jacob's Pharmacy in Atlanta, Georgia. The name "Coca-Cola" was suggested by Pemberton's bookkeeper, Frank M. Robinson, who also penned the famous Spencerian script logo.

History of Coca-Cola

The Coca-Cola Company has a rich history dating back over a century, evolving from a soda fountain drink to a global beverage giant. Key milestones include bottling, international expansion, and the introduction of new products.

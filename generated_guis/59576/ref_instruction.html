Fast Facts

Coca-Cola 101 add_circle_outline

The Coca-Cola Company: Founded in 1886, we are a total beverage company, offering over 500 brands in more than 200 countries and territories.

Our Purpose: To refresh the world and make a difference. We aim to inspire moments of optimism and happiness through our brands and actions.

Global Reach: Our portfolio includes iconic sparkling soft drinks, waters, sports drinks, juices, plant-based beverages, coffee, and tea.

Products & Ingredients add_circle_outline

Product Portfolio: From Coca-Cola to smartwater, our diverse range includes options for every taste and occasion, focusing on sugar reduction and diverse offerings.

Ingredient Transparency: We are committed to transparency about our ingredients, providing clear information on packaging and our website for informed consumer choices.

Quality Standards: All our products adhere to rigorous quality and safety standards, ensuring consistent taste and reliability globally.

Strengthening Communities add_circle_outline

Water Stewardship: We work to protect water resources, replenish what we use, and ensure sustainable water management in our operations and communities.

Economic Empowerment: Through initiatives like 5by20, we empower women entrepreneurs and support local economies where we operate.

Youth Development: We invest in programs that promote education, health, and well-being for youth, fostering future leaders and stronger communities.

Diversity & Inclusion add_circle_outline

Inclusive Culture: We foster a workplace where every employee feels valued, respected, and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives.

Global Workforce: Our diverse workforce reflects the many cultures and communities we serve around the world, driving innovation and connection.

Equal Opportunity: We are committed to providing equal opportunities for all employees and applicants, free from discrimination and bias.

Frequently Asked Questions

Well-being add_circle_outline

Q: What are Coca-Cola's healthy beverage options? A: We offer a wide range of low and no-sugar beverages across our portfolio, including Diet Coke, Coca-Cola Zero Sugar, Honest Tea, and various water brands, to help consumers manage their sugar intake.

Q: How does Coca-Cola promote active lifestyles? A: We support initiatives and programs that encourage physical activity and healthy living in communities worldwide, often partnering with local organizations to make active lifestyles accessible and enjoyable.

Q: Where can I find nutritional information for products? A: Detailed nutritional information for all our products is available on our official website, product packaging, and through various digital platforms for easy access.

Environment add_circle_outline

Q: What are Coca-Cola's sustainability goals? A: We are working towards a "World Without Waste," aiming to collect and recycle a bottle or can for every one we sell, and to make our packaging 100% recyclable.

Q: How does the company minimize its environmental footprint? A: We focus on sustainable sourcing, reducing water use, lowering carbon emissions, and increasing the use of recycled content in our packaging across our supply chain.

Q: What initiatives are in place for plastic reduction? A: We are innovating in packaging design, investing in collection infrastructure, and partnering with NGOs to develop circular economy solutions for plastic waste.

Support & Contact add_circle_outline

Q: How can I contact Coca-Cola customer service? A: You can reach our customer service team via phone, email, or through the contact form on our official website. Please visit the 'Contact Us' section for details.

Q: Where can I report a product quality issue? A: For product quality concerns, please contact our consumer affairs department immediately. Provide product details, batch codes, and purchase information for assistance.

Q: Are there resources for brand ambassadors? A: Yes, dedicated resources, guidelines, and support are available for our brand ambassadors through a specialized portal. Contact your program lead for access credentials.
